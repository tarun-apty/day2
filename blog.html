Communication 101

November 20, 2024

Click Here to jump to Paraphrase Section!

We need to be highly Assertive: Communicate clearly so that the other person is not confused
Pre-Planning is Important
If we use the word Done, it means the task is 100% completed, and nothing is left
Own your mistakes
Be clear, confident and others should understand what we are speaking


If there's no solution for the problem client is asking, hear their requirement, acknowledge, explain the process of requesting new features to be added (with this we answered and calmed down the client), talk with internal team on weekly meeting to discuss if the solution is possible. End the discussion by sending an email of the outcome. (But what if we lose a big customer in this process)

November 20, 2024
